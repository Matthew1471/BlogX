Matthew1471's Guide To Feedreader

Installation Introduction:
Feedreader allows you to read multiple blogs in a simple text format by clicking feed "folders".

Installation Requirements:
FeedReader Installed

QuickStart Instructions:
Step 1. - Drag This "Subscribe" To Your Links Toolbar or Favourites

Step 2. - Open up a Blog (Running Matthew1471's ASP BlogX)

Step 3. - Click the subscribe button you created earlier (With Feedreader running in the tray (near the clock))

That's it... Now click "Next" & "Finish" in the box which just popped up in FeedReader.. Then on your left click the Blog you wish to view.

Note : You now can do stages 2 and 3 for each Blog you wish to read on a regular basis.

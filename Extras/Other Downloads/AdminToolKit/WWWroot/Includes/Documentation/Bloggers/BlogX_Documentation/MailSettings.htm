Matthew1471 BlogX Mail Settings

Additional Information:
Click "Email Settings" in the Admin panel. (Once logged in obviously).

The page will then attempt to connect to IIS Mail components and see which are installed on your system, once the page has loaded, you will see a checkbox "Enable Email" first step is to tick this box.

Select the E-mail component from the drop down box
Note : If your e-mail component is not shown here and it is DEFINATLY installed, please contact me & I will add it.

Enter in the mail server address that you wish the site to connect to and the e-mail address you want comments to be sent to.

Click save and users can now send you comments.
Toggle "Enable Email" on or off to turn the comments on or off.
